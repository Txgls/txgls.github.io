📋 Commands

Admin Commands

  • /clans chatspy- enables/disables the chat spy to view other clan chats
  • /clans reload- reloads the configuration file

Clan Management Commands

  • /clans create (clan name)- creates a clan of your choosing
  • /clans leave- leaves your clan
  • /clans view (player)- views which clan that player is in
  • /clans who (clan name)- displays the members of that clan
  • /clans kick (player)- kicks the player from the clan (leader command)
  • /clans invite (player)- invites the player to your clan (leader command)
  • /clans accept/decline- accepts or declines a clan invitation
  • /clans list- lists all active clans on the server
  • /clans leader- promotes another to clan leader

Communication Commands

  • /clans chat (message)- sends a message in your clan chat

Teleportation Commands

  • /clans warp (warp name)- warps to a clan warp
  • /clans setwarp (warp name)- sets the warp to your location (leader command)
  • /clans delwarp (warp name)- deletes the warp (leader command)
  • /clans home- teleports to the clans home
  • /clans sethome- sets the clan home (leader command)
  • /clans delhome- deletes the clan home (leader command)

Banking Commands

  • /clans bank- opens up the clan bank

🔐 Permissions

  • clans.base- base command to use the clans commands (default: true)
  • clans.admin- access to administrator commands (op: default)

📢 Notice

All permissions are defaulted except for clans.admin. This means most features are accessible to all players by default, with only administrative functions requiring special permissions.
